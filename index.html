About the band first years

In 1984, ht eyoungers Dexter Holland and Greg K, were school gym friends in Huntigton Beach, they decide to create a new band after leaving a local project concert called Social Distortion.

Later, Kevin "Noodles" Wasserman was invited to be a part of the band because his infamous talent to have alcohol for free for band members, who were minors at the time.

Playing under the Mannic Subsidal name, in 1985 the band decide to switch the name to "The Offsrping". Its first single, "I'll Be Waiting/Blackball", was released in the vinyl format by Black Label Records with 1,000 copy sales.

Its main album, The Offspring, was launched by Nemesis Records in 1989 and produced by Thom Wilson selling over 1,000 vinyl copies
